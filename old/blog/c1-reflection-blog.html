Reflections

What's your take on the DBC/EDA experience?

It's a great sort of environment. To see people working together to improve their learning is wonderful. Where everyone in the group wants to be there and improve themselves. I really enjoy this small group setting, where the tutors want to see you succeed as much as we do.

What are your impressions?

It's all about what you put into it. And it's going to be fun.

How do you see yourself engaging with this type of culture?

I think it will go great. I love being able to work with others, to be able to work collaboratively. I have always enjoyed being able to assist others when they need it (and get that little help with mine when I'm stuck), so while I know I can be shy at first, once I'm in there and doing the things it'll be a good time. It's something that I'm a bit nervous, just due to it being a totally new environment, but I know that as soon as we get into it I'll really enjoy it and get into it.

Have your expectations of EDA changed? If so, how?

No, I don't believe they have. I did a decent amount of research before I signed up so had a reasonable sort of picture of what to expect.

Are you excited to participate in this kind of learning environment? Does it make you nervous?

Yes and yes.
